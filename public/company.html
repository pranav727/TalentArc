About Stripe

Stripe is a financial infrastructure platform for businesses. Millions of companies—from the world's largest enterprises to the most ambitious startups—use Stripe to accept payments, grow their revenue, and accelerate new business opportunities.

Founded in 2010 by brothers Patrick and John Collison, Stripe has grown to become one of the most valuable private technology companies in the world. The company processes hundreds of billions of dollars annually for businesses in 195+ countries.

With a presence in over 40 countries and a team of more than 8,500 people, Stripe combines the focus and agility of a startup with the resources and reach of a global enterprise. Our engineering and product teams tackle ambitious, long-term problems in finance, infrastructure, and tooling.

Our Mission

"Increase the GDP of the internet. We want to make it easy to build companies and monetize products, so that more of the world's ambitions can become realities."
